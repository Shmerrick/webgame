Players may choose ONE crafting profession.

Core Gear Professions

Armor Smithing
Creates metal and leather armors (helmets, chestplates, greaves, boots, gauntlets).
Weapon Smithing
Creates melee weapons (swords, axes, maces, daggers, polearms).
Bowyer
Creates bows, crossbows, arrows, bolts.
Shield Crafting
Creates shields of various sizes and styles.

Consumables & Enhancements

Alchemy
Potions, poisons, elixirs, antidotes.
Cooking
Food buffs, meals, feasts for groups.
Enchanting
Runes, wards, permanent magical buffs (e.g., stamina regen, mana efficiency).
Artificer
Imbues non-magical gear with elemental damage (fire sword, frost arrows) or magical defense (resistance wards).

Luxury

Jewelry Crafting
Rings, necklaces, amulets, trinkets (no socketing, no augmenting).
