7. Magical Defense System

Magic defense is active, unlike passive armor defense.

  • Elemental Counters ‚Üí cast opposite element to weaken/erase.
  • Entropy Barriers ‚Üí Radiance disperses, Void absorbs.
  • Null Fields (Void) ‚Üí suppress or weaken magic in zones.
  • Counter-Casting ‚Üí time a cast to cancel enemy spells.
