Developer Summary

1. Core Concept

  • Ascension is a temporary divine elevation granted by the elemental gods.
  • Void = entropy reduction (stability, order, decay).
  • Radiance = entropy increase (chaos, volatility, disruption).
  • Neither is good nor evil ‚Äî both are natural cosmic forces.
  • Ascension lasts 24 hours, with possible short extensions under certain conditions.
  • Only player-vs-player (PvP) kills contribute to gaining or maintaining Ascension.

2. Types of Ascension

üåå Elemental Ascension (Divine Choosing)

  • Void Ascended (Elemental): Gain power by systematically killing players. Buffs (24h): +20% All Damage, Elemental Immunity. Penalty: ‚Äì20% to all stats for 24h if killed before expiration. Cooldown: 3 real days.
  • Radiance Ascended (Elemental): Gained by being the top damage contributor against a Void Ascended player. Buffs (24h): +20% Damage Reduction, Elemental Immunity. Penalty: None.

‚öîÔ∏è Tribunal Ascension (Non-Elemental Choosing)

  • Void Tribunal Ascended: Requirement: Reach a kill threshold (e.g., 100 player kills). Buff (24h): +20% Physical Damage.
  • Radiance Tribunal Ascended: Requirement: Defeat a Void Tribunal Ascended in PvP. Buff (24h): +20% Damage Reduction.
  • Shared Ability (Both Tribunal Types): Weapon Imbuement (10 min duration, choose any elemental effect).

3. Intelligent Damage Tracking

Radiance Ascension is awarded to the player who dealt the most damage to a Void Ascended during their entire 24h reign. If that player is offline, it goes to the next highest online contributor.

4. Buff Decay & Maintenance

  • After 12 hours, their flat attribute boost is removed.
  • Their damage bonus/reduction begins to decay by 2% per hour unless they score PvP kills.

5. Anti-Exploitation Safeguards

  • No farming alts or guildmates.
  • Godly Smite: Cheaters are stripped of status and receive a divine debuff.

6. Time Extensions

  • In the final 2 hours, players may slightly extend their reign through continued PvP kills (max 1-2 hours).
