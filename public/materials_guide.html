Material and Gameplay System

How real-world materials are translated into gameplay statistics.

1. Overview

The system is divided into four material families: Metals, Organics, Rocks, and Minerals. Each family has a Tier system (1–5), where Tier 5 represents mythical materials with a ~10% enhancement over Tier 4.

2. Real-World Material Properties

Each material is registered with baseline scientific properties which are then normalized to a 0-1 scale for gameplay calculations.

  • Density (g/cm³)
  • Hardness (Mohs or Janka)
  • Elastic Modulus (GPa) – stiffness
  • Thermal & Electrical Conductivity
  • Toughness & Chemical Resistance
  • Water Absorption

3. Gameplay Formulas

The following formulas translate normalized real-world properties into gameplay values.

Offense Formulas

Offense_Slash = 0.6 × Hardness_norm + 0.4 × Density_norm Offense_Pierce = 0.6 × Hardness_norm + 0.4 × ElasticMod_norm Offense_Blunt = 0.6 × Density_norm + 0.4 × Toughness_norm

Defense Formulas

Defense_Slash = 0.55 × Hardness_norm + 0.45 × Toughness_norm Defense_Pierce = 0.55 × Hardness_norm + 0.45 × ElasticMod_norm Defense_Blunt = 0.55 × (1 − ElasticMod_norm) + 0.45 × Toughness_norm

Elemental Resistance Formulas

Element_Fire = 0.7 × (1 − ThermalCond_norm) + 0.3 × ChemRes_norm Element_Water = ChemRes_norm × (1 − 0.5 × WaterAbsorption_rel) Element_Wind = 1 − ElectricalCond_norm Element_Earth = 0.5 × Toughness_norm + 0.5 × Density_norm

Environmental Formula

Heat_Retention = 0.6 × (1 − ThermalCond_norm) + 0.4 × Density_norm

5. Melee Weapon Damage Model

This section defines how to compute melee damage for unimbued and elementally imbued weapons.

Unimbued (Physical-Only) Damage

Base damage is distributed across physical types (Slash, Pierce, Blunt) using normalized weights derived from the weapon head's material properties.

Phys_Slash = Base_Dmg × Weight_Slash Phys_Pierce = Base_Dmg × Weight_Pierce Phys_Blunt = Base_Dmg × Weight_Blunt

Elementally Imbued Damage (Additive)

When an element is applied, the full physical damage is kept, and an elemental damage term is added. This term is scaled by a global tuning factor for the element, an optional power multiplier from the spell, and the weapon head's elemental affinity.

Elem_Damage = Base_Dmg × Tuning_Factor × Imbue_Power × Head_Affinity Total_Payload = { Physical_Damage, Elemental_Damage }
